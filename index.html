About Us

At Chop's Mobile Detailing, we bring professional-grade car care directly to your home or workplace across the 757 Hampton Roads area.

Known for reliable service, attention to detail, and showroom-quality results, we specialize in interior and exterior detailing, paint correction, shampooing, ceramic coatings, and premium hand washes.
